Lorena León

Assistant Professor

GIPSA-lab

Université Grenoble Alpes

I am an Assistant Professor in Computer Science at Université Grenoble Alpes and the GIPSA-lab.

I received my B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees in Mathematics from the University of Havana (Havana, Cuba). In 2022, I obtained my Ph.D. in Signal Processing from the National Polytechnic Institute of Toulouse (Toulouse, France).

From March 2023 to August 2025, I was a Postdoctoral Research Associate with the Ultrasound Imaging Group at CREATIS and Université Claude Bernard Lyon 1 (Lyon, France).

Since December 2024, I have been a member of the EURASIP Academy, a program that supports early-career researchers in the field of signal processing.

News & Updates
